About me

A bit of context on who I am and how I work.

My name is Willy, though the community knows me as willymax. I live in Barcelona and I've spent a good amount of time in game development, mostly on Roblox, though I also have a background in the Minecraft network world.

My English is at a solid, advanced level, so working with international teams or supporting players from other countries has never been an issue for me.

I'm easygoing and approachable within a team. If something breaks, I'd rather look into it as soon as I can, even if it's late for me, because I know how important it is to fix issues quickly. I put in a lot of hours on every project I join, and that's the mindset I bring to any new team.
